Echoes of the Unseen

Step Lightly — The World Is Listening

Echoes of the Unseen is the ultimate stealth-and-tracking plugin for Minecraft servers seeking a new dimension of tension and strategy. Every step you take leaves behind a fleeting trace—breadcrumbs that can be discovered by vigilant players looking to stalk your every move.

Experience heart-pounding PvP where distant foes vanish from sight until they cross your threshold, turning every encounter into a white-knuckle surprise. Hunt, be hunted, and remember: there's no such thing as truly hiding when the world is listening.

Ready to take your server to the next level? Grab Echoes of the Unseen and transform ordinary survival or faction play into a thrilling game of cat and mouse!

Who Will You Be?

The Shadow Broker

Weave a web of secrets and deals. Gather intel on factions and players, and barter confidential information for profit or influence. Whether you trade alliances or instigate betrayals, the Shadow Broker thrives on intrigue and clandestine negotiations.

The Marauder

Ambush, raid, and strike fear. Use the plugin’s breadcrumb trails to pinpoint prey, strike swiftly, and vanish before enemies mobilize. High-risk, high-reward—tread carefully.

The Pathfinder

Chart the unknown for profit. Venture into distant realms to locate precious resources, then sell your discoveries—or guard them for personal gain. Knowledge is power when every hidden corner holds treasure.

The Overseer

Build mighty fortresses, defend vast stores of wealth, and establish robust supply lines. Turn your domain into an unassailable haven, where wealth fuels power and power secures your empire.

The Phantom

A solitary ghost who slips through the night unseen. Maximize stealth, minimize breadcrumbs, and thrive by avoiding direct conflicts. Live by your wits, trusting no one—nor needing anyone.

Something Else?

Forge your own path! Combine stealth, cunning, raw power, or any creative approach to thrive in this dynamic realm of footprints and hidden clues. Your style, your story—this is Echoes of the Unseen.
